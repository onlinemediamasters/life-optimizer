Vault Items

About this vault
Encryption: AES-256-GCM with PBKDF2 key derivation (250,000 iterations). Industry standard.
Storage: Encrypted blob in browser localStorage. Included in JSON exports as ciphertext — safe to back up.
Recovery: None. There is no "forgot password" option. The password and the encrypted data are the only inputs to decryption.
Best practice: For things like birth certificate location, safe codes, recovery codes — great. For highly sensitive items (full SSN, account passwords), use a dedicated password manager like 1Password or Bitwarden.

What's HSA Eligible?
Comprehensive reference
YES — Medical Care
  • Doctor visits, copays, deductibles (all specialties)
  • Urgent care, ER visits, ambulance rides
  • Dental: cleanings, fillings, crowns, root canals, orthodontia, dentures, mouth guards (medical), dental sealants, fluoride treatments
  • Vision: eye exams, glasses (frames + lenses), prescription sunglasses, contact lenses, contact solution, LASIK / PRK surgery, reading glasses (if prescribed), vision therapy
  • Mental health: therapy, psychiatry, psychology, marriage counseling (if medically necessary), group therapy
  • Chiropractor, acupuncture, osteopath
  • Physical therapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy
  • Surgery (in/outpatient), hospital stays, anesthesia
  • Lab tests, X-rays, MRI, CT scans, ultrasounds, EKG/EEG
  • Pregnancy: prenatal care, childbirth, midwife, doula (some)
  • Fertility: IVF, IUI, egg freezing, sperm banking
  • Annual physicals, vaccinations (incl. travel vaccines)
  • Allergy testing & shots, allergy serum
  • Substance abuse treatment, rehab programs
  • Smoking cessation programs
  • Skin cancer screening, dermatology, mole removal
  • Hearing tests, hearing aid fittings
YES — OTC Medications & Health Items
  • Prescription medications (any)
  • OTC pain relievers: Tylenol, Advil, Aleve, aspirin
  • OTC allergy meds: Claritin, Zyrtec, Allegra, Benadryl, Flonase
  • Cold/flu/sinus: NyQuil, DayQuil, Sudafed, Mucinex, throat lozenges
  • Antacids/heartburn: Tums, Pepcid, Prilosec OTC, Zantac
  • Anti-diarrheal/laxatives: Imodium, MiraLAX, Pepto-Bismol
  • Anti-itch/hydrocortisone creams, calamine, Cortizone
  • Eye drops (Visine, Refresh, Systane), ear drops
  • Sleep aids: ZzzQuil, Unisom (OTC)
  • Motion sickness: Dramamine, Bonine
  • Insulin, glucose monitors, test strips, lancets, CGMs
  • Inhalers, EpiPens, nebulizers
  • Nicotine patches, gum, lozenges
  • Menstrual products: pads, tampons, cups, period underwear, panty liners
  • Sunscreen SPF 15+ broad spectrum — ANY brand, drugstore or prescription. Includes sport sunscreens, mineral sunscreens, kids' sunscreens, sunscreen sticks/sprays/lotions. After-sun aloe with SPF.
  • Lip balms with SPF 15+
  • Diaper rash cream, baby gas drops, baby acetaminophen
  • Bandages, gauze, antiseptic (Neosporin, iodine, hydrogen peroxide)
  • Hot/cold therapy: ice packs, heating pads, IcyHot, Tiger Balm, BenGay
  • First aid kits, ace bandages, KT tape (medical)
  • Thermometers (oral, forehead, ear), blood pressure monitors, pulse oximeters
  • Pregnancy tests, ovulation tests, COVID home tests
  • Condoms, contraceptives, plan B
  • Reading glasses (non-prescription, +1 to +3)
  • Lice treatments, scabies treatments
  • Sunburn relief, wart removers, corn/callus removers
  • Acne treatments (Rx + OTC like Differin, salicylic acid)
  • Cough drops/throat sprays (Halls, Chloraseptic)
YES — Equipment & Specialty
  • CPAP/BiPAP machines, masks, hoses, filters, distilled water for CPAP
  • Crutches, walkers, canes, wheelchairs, scooters
  • Hearing aids, batteries, cleaning supplies
  • Braces, splints, slings, supports, orthotics (custom or OTC), arch supports
  • Compression stockings/socks (medical grade)
  • Breast pumps, milk storage bags, nursing pads, lactation supplies
  • Smart rings/watches (Oura, etc.) — if prescribed for medical purpose with LMN
  • Air purifiers/HEPA filters — if prescribed (asthma/allergies, LMN)
  • Humidifiers (Vicks, etc. for cough/cold — eligible without LMN)
  • Light therapy lamps for seasonal affective disorder (LMN)
  • TENS units for pain relief
  • Diabetic shoes & inserts (with prescription)
  • Wigs (if hair loss due to disease)
  • Service animal care (for diagnosed conditions)
  • Home modifications for medical needs (ramps, grab bars)
  • Travel costs for medical care (mileage, lodging up to limit)
  • Special education tutoring (for learning disabilities with diagnosis)
  • Lead-based paint removal (if for child with lead poisoning)
NO — Not Eligible (or needs LMN)
  • Gym memberships (even with LMN, often denied)
  • General multivitamins & daily vitamins
  • Vitamin D, calcium, iron, magnesium — need Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN) for diagnosed deficiency
  • Fish oil / omega-3, glucosamine, probiotics — need LMN for specific condition
  • Melatonin — needs LMN for sleep disorder
  • Protein powder, BCAAs, creatine — need LMN (e.g., post-surgery recovery)
  • Wellness supplements: collagen, greens powders, mushroom blends, adaptogens
  • Pre-workout, weight gainers, fat burners
  • Prenatal vitamins are the ONE exception — always eligible
  • CBD/THC products (only if prescribed in legal state with LMN)
  • Cosmetic procedures: Botox (unless medical), fillers, cosmetic surgery
  • Teeth whitening (in-office or strips)
  • Hair restoration: Rogaine, hair transplants, Propecia (for cosmetic)
  • Health insurance premiums (mostly — exceptions: COBRA, Medicare Part B/D, LTC)
  • Toothbrushes (manual or electric), toothpaste, mouthwash, floss
  • Cosmetic dentistry (veneers for appearance, whitening)
  • Maternity/nursing clothes
  • Personal trainers (even with LMN, often denied)
  • Yoga, pilates, group fitness classes
  • Diet food, meal replacements, Weight Watchers (unless LMN for obesity)
  • Massage therapy (needs LMN for back pain, injury)
  • Acupuncture is eligible, but supplements from acupuncturist are not
  • Bottled water, electrolyte drinks (Gatorade)
  • Aromatherapy oils, essential oils
  • Funeral expenses, burial plots
  • Marijuana (federally illegal so never HSA-eligible)
  • Pet medical care (unless service animal for diagnosed condition)
Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN): Many items become HSA-eligible if your doctor writes a brief letter stating you need them for a diagnosed medical condition. The letter should include: your diagnosis, the recommended product/service, duration of need, and doctor's signature. Keep these letters for 7 years with your receipts.

How Life Tracker Works
Architecture, data flow, and what Claude does

Where data lives

Everything you enter is stored in your browser's localStorage on this device. Nothing is sent to a server. Closing the tab does not delete data, but clearing browser data does. Use Settings → Export Backup regularly to save a JSON copy.

Uploaded files

Statements (PDF, CSV, XLSX) and images are parsed in your browser. The extracted transaction data is saved, but the original files are not stored — they're discarded after parsing. Keep originals in Google Drive or your computer.

Where Claude is used

  • Statement parsing — for PDFs/images that don't extract cleanly locally, Claude reads the file and pulls out transactions
  • Categorization — Claude assigns Schedule C / Chase categories to transactions
  • Insights tab — generates the full financial analysis (spending breakdown, fee alerts, recommendations)
  • Card benefits — "Check Missing Benefits" uses Claude + web search to find current credit card credits and expirations
  • Card offers — reads screenshots of Chase/Amex Offers and matches to your spending
  • Find Better Cards — compares your cards to current market alternatives with sign-up bonuses
  • Brokerage — "Refresh Prices" looks up current quotes; "Run Analysis" compares managed vs DIY
  • Asset value check — looks up recent comps for collectibles you're tracking
  • Tax returns — extracts year, AGI, Schedule C from prior returns
  • Vehicle maintenance — pulls the factory schedule for your specific car
  • Smart Shopper — product recommendations + which of your cards earns most

API key

You add your own Anthropic API key in Settings. Calls go directly from your browser to api.anthropic.com. No backend server. Roughly $0.01-0.05 per Claude action depending on size.

How numbers are populated

  • Dashboard tiles — if 1-2 months of statements are uploaded, projects using last year's tax return. 3-11 months: extrapolates current pace. 12+ months or no return: uses actual data.
  • Renewals — auto-syncs from credit card annual fees + benefit expirations + detected recurring charges from transactions. Manual adds also supported.
  • Categorized expenses — transactions tagged Business+negative go to Schedule C buckets. Personal+negative go to Chase-style buckets.
  • Stock chart history — builds one data point per day each time you click "Refresh Prices"
